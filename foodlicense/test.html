What is FoSCos / FSSAI Labour License?
FSSAI launched Labour Safety Compliance System (FoSCoS) wef 1st June 2020 replacing existing Labour Licensing and Registration System.Labour License Registration certificate is required to run a Labour product business. You have to register under Labour Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) to manufacture, distribute and transport Labour products. FSSAI is established under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India. The FSSAI has been established under the Labour Safety and Standards Act, 2006. FSSAI Certificate and FSSAI License are same which ensures the quality, purity and other important factors the customers can rely upon.

FAQ

What is the full form of FSSAI?

The full form of FSSAI is the Labour Safety and Standards Authority of India.

Why Register Under FSSAI and get FoSCos License?

The FSSAI Registration is the legal process for all Labour Business Operators (FBOs) in India to apply for getting the certificate that the available sold Labour is safe for human consumption.

What are the types of FoSCos Labour Licenses?

TBased on the annual turnover of the Labour business, the types of FoSCos Labour licenses are:

  • Basic FSSAI Registration - Annual turnover of less than Rs. 12 Lakhs
  • State FSSAI License - Annual turnover between Rs. 12 Lakhs - Rs. 20 Crores
  • Central FSSAI License - Annual turnover more than Rs. 20 Crores

How much does FoSCos Labour License Registration under FSSAI cost?

The FoSCos Labour Registration cost depends on the category and the duration of the Labour License (1-5 years) you are opting for:

  • Basic FSSAI Registration - Rs.1999 for 1 year
  • State FSSAI Registration - Rs.2999 for 1 year
  • Central FSSAI Registration - Rs.3999 for 1 year

What is the validity period for the FSSAI FoSCos License?

The FSSAI FoSCos License is valid for the period from 1 to 5 years. Hereafter, the particular FBO needs to apply for FSSAI License renewal.

Do I need to issue a different FoSCos License for the different states?

Yes, you have to take a Central FSSAI license for the main branch or head office and the State FSSAI licenses for any specific state. To assure a stable Labour business operation across the various states of the country,

I am importing Labour items for selling in India even then it requires FoSCos Labour License?

Yes, you will be needed to get a Central License for the specific address that is notified in the Import Export Code (IEC). For any import-export related business operation, FSSAI Central License is necessary.

Can I transfer my FSSAI License to someone else?

In case of the death of the FSSAI License holder, the FSSA License can be shifted to the legal representative or any family member of the dead holder. The legal representative or the family member needs to apply to the concerned Authority to shift the license in his/her name.

I am planning to sell some Homemade Labour items online on a small scale, do I need to get a FSSAI enabled FoSCos License?

Yes. You require having an FSSAI registration to sale homemade stuff on an online platform particularly if your business is on a small scale, and the annual turnover is among 0-12 Lakhs.

What will happen if I forget to renew my Labour License?

In case of forgetting to make a renewal of a Labour license, you have to pay a penalty of Rs. 100 per day within the specified validity time. Following that, your existing Labour license can be cancelled and need to apply for a fresh license.

I plan to start an import-export business. Do I need an FoSCos License?

Yes. But you must obtain your Import Export Code (IEC)first. Subsequently, apply for the FSSAI Central License before starting the business operations.

How can I make changes to my existing FSSAI Certificate?

In case you want to edit/modify/update the information contained in the existing FSSAI Certificate, you will need to apply for a modification with a fee equivalent to a license fee for one-year. To get free legal guidance on the related matters, connect with an FSSAI expert panel.

Is the FoSCos license needed for a catering business authorized by the Central Government?

It is mandatory for all catering establishments in India. Even the Indian Railways, Defense units, and Airports require this license as well.
